
# Accessibility Statement

_Last updated: 28 March 2026_

Food Passport is committed to making our website and app accessible to as many people as possible. We are continually working to improve the user experience and welcome feedback on accessibility.

## Website Accessibility

We aim to follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 AA standard as much as possible. Measures we take include:
- Use of semantic HTML
- Sufficient color contrast
- Keyboard navigation support
- Alt text for images

We regularly review our site to improve accessibility.

## App Accessibility

At this time, we have not specifically designed the app to meet accessibility standards, but we plan to review and improve accessibility in the future.

## Feedback

If you experience any difficulty accessing content or features in our website or app, please contact us at: hello@foodpassportuk.co.uk

We value your feedback and will do our best to address any accessibility issues.
